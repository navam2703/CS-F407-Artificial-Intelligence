Let us see how you can pursue a career in research.
The educational qualifications you need to fulfill to become a researcher is a master's degree(research-focused) or Ph.D. in your field of interest. 
Further, prior work experience as a research associate or working on various research projects is also preferable.
You can enroll in any technical club which focuses on your area of interest.
Some examples are IEEE, ACM, ARC, CRUx
Maintaining a high CGPA is of utmost importance. A CGPA above 9 will make for a strong profile.
Lower CGPA can be offset by projects or other research experience.
Post graduate exams like GRE and GATE should be focussed on.
